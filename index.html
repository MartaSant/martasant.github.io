About Marta

Marta Rossi is an Italian food scientist specializing in food production processes, food waste, and sustainable supply chains. She holds a Laurea Magistrale in Food Science and Technology (LM-70) from the University of Bologna, one of Europe‚Äôs leading institutions in agricultural and food sciences, where her studies focused on food processing technologies, quality control, EU food law, and environmental impacts of the agri-food sector.

On Food Facts, Marta translates scientific literature, EU regulations, and production data into accessible, evidence-based articles. She documents how food is grown, processed, packaged and distributed ‚Äì and why, in a world where roughly one third of food produced for human consumption is lost or wasted, rethinking production and overproduction is crucial for both climate and food security.
