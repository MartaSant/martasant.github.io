Hi, I'm Marta, the curious mind behind Food Facts.

I created this space to talk about what’s really behind what we eat: ingredients, nutrition, labels, and the processes that turn raw crops into the food on our shelves.

Here you’ll find simple explanations about food production, how different foods are made, what terms on packaging actually mean, and how to make more informed choices without fear or perfectionism.

If you’re curious about how food is grown, processed, packed, and brought to your table, you’re in the right place.
